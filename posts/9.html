    • Campylobacter

      • cross-contamination at slaughter
      • undercooked chicken or cross-contamination to sth raw
    • Norwalk-like virus
      • vomiting with diarrhea (couple of days)
      • no animal host
      • passed person-to-person
    • Salmonella
      • animal guts
        • birds
        • mammels
        • reptiles
      • diarrhea, fever, cramps
    • E Coli O157:H7
      • bloody diarrhea
      • severe cramping
      • not much fever
      • mammalian gut
        • animal manvre
      • 5% hemodytic remia syndrone
        • profuse bleeding
        • kidney failure
        • paralysis
    • Listeria
      • infants
      • elderly
      • compromized immune system
      • muscleaches
      • pregnancy -> pass to newborn
    • other routes - cryptosporidia - giardia - shigella - hepatitis A
