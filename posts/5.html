    Linear Congruences

    $ax \equiv b (mod m)$
    m integer, $a,b \in Z$

    a solution is some x that satisfies the equation

    $x = \frac {a} {b}$

    there are inverses:
    a’ is inverse of a, if $a * a’ \equiv 1 (mod m)$
