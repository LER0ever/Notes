    • Campylobacter

      • vomiting with diarrhea (couple of days)
      • no animal host
      • passed person-to-person
    • Salmonella
      • animal guts
        • birds
        • mammels
        • reptiles
      • diarrhea, fever, cramps
    • E Coli O157:H7
      • bloody diarrhea
      • severe cramping
      • not much fever
      • mammalian gut
        • animal manvre
      • 5% hemodytic remia syndrone
        • profuse bleeding
        • kidney failure
        • paralysis
    • Listeria
      • infants
      • elderly
      • compromized immune system
      • muscleaches
      • pregnancy -> pass to newborn
    • other routes - cryptosporidia - giardia - shigella - hepatitis A

    Foodborne Toxins

    • Batulism
      • paralysis
    • Staplylococcus
      • vomitting
    • Dangers
      • batulism - fatal
      • hemolytic nremia syndrone
        • nervous system paralysis
        • kidney failure
      • any severe if
        • infant
        • elderly
        • compromized immune system

    Doctor if

    • prolonged vomiting --> dehydration
    • diarrhea > 3days || bloody

    Sources

    • animal guts
      • mammals
      • birds
      • reptils

    • raw unpasteurized milk
    • Food that pool animals
      • ground beef
      • bulk raw milk
      • raw eggs
      • F/V washed or irrigated with manure contaminated water
        • manure can be safe if composted
        • organic same risk as non-organic
    • Sprouts
    • unpasteurized fruit juice
    • infected food handlers

    Prevention

    • Regulatory

        • roasts - steaks: 145 F

        • Pork: 160 F
