    • $\uparrow$ breath

    • $\uparrow$ energy demand
    • large muscle group

    • rhythmic repetitive

    • Cardiovascular benefits

    Resistance(strength) exercise

    • strength
    • $\downarrow$ energy demand

    Training Principle

    • apply stress
    • rest - recovery
    • body adapts
    • repeat

    Aerobic

    Frequency
    • ” most days “
    • at least 3 times / week
    Intensity
    • Cardiovascular benefit
    • energy system
    Max Heart Rate 220-age

    60-80% of max HR

    Time
    • “About an hour”
    • at least 20 min
    Progression
    • 10% rule
    Injury
    • Warmup
    • Apply too much stress
      • Reduce stress
    • Technique issue
    • Equipment issue

    Life Long Exercise

    • Useful
      • commutins
      • Mowing
    • Recreation
    • Social

      • Obsessive thoughts
      • Depression

      • 1994
      • 15% of college women

      • early adult
    • Feature
      • Stress-induced binges

    • Complications

      • Bloating
      • Hyperthermia
      • chronic diarrhea or constipation
      • obesity
        • type 2 diabetes

      • psychotherapy

      • exercise
      • nutritional counseling
      • drug treatment
        • anti-depressants
