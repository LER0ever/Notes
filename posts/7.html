    Energy Balance

    Intake = expenditure
    $\uparrow$ weight $\to$ $\uparrow$ expenditure
    $\downarrow$ weight $\to$ $\downarrow$ expenditure

    20 genes linked to obesity

    obesity - interaction between genes + environment

    Losing weight

    Negative energy balance

    • $\downarrow$ Intake
    • $\uparrow$ expenditure
    Not sustainable
    • Portion size restriction (hunger)
    • variety restriction (monotony)
    • Meal frequency
    • Lower Metabolism
      • brain needs glucose
      • gluconeogenesis
      • lean mass --> glucose
      • $\downarrow$ BMR
    $\downarrow$ intake, $\uparrow$ expenditure

    $\uparrow$ expenditure

    • Physical Activity
      • daily activity
      • exercise
    AerobicResistance(strength)
    $\uparrow$ breathing$\uparrow$ lean mass
    $\uparrow$ heart rate $\to$ $\uparrow$ expenditure$\uparrow$ BMR

    Maintaining Metabolism**

    • $\downarrow$ loss of lean mass
    • prevent gluconeogenesis
    • Regular Carbohydrate-containting meals
    • Don’t go hungry too long
    • At least 3 meals/day
    • At least 2-5 hours
    • Car has a proteins-sparing effect
