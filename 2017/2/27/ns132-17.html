    Binge Eating Disorder

    • recurrent binges
      • Excessive abnormal amount
      • discrete period of time
      • high calories, easily ingested food
      • sense of loss of control
      • inability to stop
      • stopped by pain, sleep, interruption
      • Higher body weight
      • Obsessive thoughts
      • Depression
    • History
      • 1994
      • 15% of college women
      • some of the obese
      • some of the depressed
    • Onset
      • early adult
    • Feature
      • Stress-induced binges

    • Complications

      • Chronic Heartburn
      • Bloating
      • Hyperthermia
      • chronic diarrhea or constipation
      • obesity
        • type 2 diabetes

    • Treatment

      • psychotherapy
      • behavior modification
      • exercise
      • nutritional counseling
      • drug treatment
        • anti-depressants
