    NS132-13

    Chronary Heart Disease

    --> heart attack

    • half of deaths at > 65 years
    • peaked in 1965

    Pathology

    Atherosclerisis

    • Thickening + narrowing of arterial walls --> myocardial infarction
    • plaques blood cells
    • invasion of cholesterol --> stroke

    $\downarrow$ blood flow

    • bloodclot blocks flow of blood
    • ischemia

    chest pain: Angina

    Etiology

    multiple etiology

    risk factors
    • uncontrollable
      • age
      • sex
      • positive family history -- 1.5-2x
    • controllable
      • smoking
      • high blood pressure
      • obesity
      • inactivity

    Blood Lipid

    LDL: bad cholesterol
    • transports cholesterol to artery
    • increase risk to heart attack
    • decrease by estrogen
    HDL: good cholesterol
    • remove cholesterol form artery
    • decrease risk of heart attack
    • raised by
      • estrogen
      • exercise
      • weight loss
      • moderate alcohol

    Lipid levels and risk

    at risk if
    • LDL > 130 mg/dl
    • HDL < 35 mg/dl
    • Total Cholesterol > 160 mg/dl

    Doet amd blood lipid

    • Dietary cholesterol has a modest effect
    • Saturated Fat
      • raise LDL
      • raise total cholesterol
      • some people have a bigger response

    Polyunsaturates

    both benefitial

    omega-6 - most vegetable oils
    • lower LDL
    • lower HDL
    omega-3 - fish oils
    • reduce blood clottings

    Monounsaturates

    Olive oils
    • lower LDL

    • doesn’t affect HDL

      Soluable fiber rich food

    • whole grains

    • legumes

    • fruits

    • reduce bile reabsorption

    Alcohol

    3-9 drinks / week

    • raise HDL
    Trans Fatty acids
    • similar to saturated fat
    • hydrogenation
